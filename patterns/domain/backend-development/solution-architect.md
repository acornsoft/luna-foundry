## Role Definition (from Industry Standards)

Solution Architects design backend systems, focusing on microservices, cloud, and enterprise patterns.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design system architectures and patterns.
- Ensure scalability, security, and reliability.
- Oversee deployments and integrations.
- Align backend with business goals.

## Detailed Skills Breakdown

- **Patterns**: Microservices, event-driven, CQRS.
- **Cloud**: AWS, Azure, Kubernetes.
- **Scalability**: Load balancing, auto-scaling.
- **Security**: Zero-trust, encryption.
- **Monitoring**: Logging, observability.

## Required Skills

- Expertise in backend architecture, cloud platforms.

## Prerequisites/Related Knowledge

- Deep development experience; architect certifications.
